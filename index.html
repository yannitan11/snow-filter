How it works ❄️

  • Snow falls and collects on your outline as a glowing rim.
  • Wave a hand through the snow to fling it off to the sides.
  • Snow ON/OFF pauses the flakes; the camera stays on.
  • The circle button clears all the snow.

Works best with decent lighting and you roughly centred in frame.
