How it works ❄️

  • It falls and collects on your outline as a glowing rim.
  • Wave a hand through it to fling it off to the sides.
  • Make a fist nearby to squeeze it into a ball…
  • …then open your palm to throw it — flick as you release!
  • Pull the cord (top-left) to change season: ❄️ 🌸 ☀️ 🍂
  • ON/OFF pauses it; the circle button clears everything.

Works best with decent lighting and you roughly centred in frame.
